Read more, buy less

We are a group of web developers who seek for a textbook sharing platform from where every student can benefit. Students often purchase textbooks at high price, but end up leaving them unused on bookshelves for ages. On the other hand, there are people who wishes to use a book only for a short period of time. Our ideal is to create a site for these users; book holders who are not going to touch their book for a while can lend out to the others who needs it temporaily and also make money in the progress.
